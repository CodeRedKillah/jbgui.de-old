Installation (with computer)


This is the preferred method of installing jailbreak apps onto one's device, but there is a method to do it without a computer if needed. If you have a computer, then read on.


To get started, you'll need to install Cydia Impactor on your computer. Simply click the link and install it like any other program. Next, you will need the ipa for Chimera from The Chimera website. Once you have it downloaded, plug your device into your computer, open Impactor and check if your device is seen. It should show as <Device Name> [<UUID>].


Then, go to the menu and choose Device --> Install Package. A file browser should open. Select the ipa for Chimera, then follow the instructions. If you run into any errors after entering your Apple ID, read our page on Impactor.

Once the progressbar and text under the menus disappear with no errors, Chimera should be installed on your device. Before you open it, Head to settings, go to General --> Profiles & Device Management and tap the button with your Apple ID email. Press the blue trust button, and you should now be able to open the app.


Installation (no computer)


Note: This method only works when a signing service has a valid certificate.


On your apple device, navigate to jailbreaks.fun in Safari. Scroll down until you find Chimera. Tap on it, then press the blue "GET" button.

After waiting a few seconds a popup should appear asking you if you want to install it. Press install. In a few seconds the app should be on your home screen.

Before you open it, open settings, go to General --> Profiles & Device Management and tap the developer under the heading "ENTERPRISE APP".  Press the blue trust button, and you should now be able to open the app.

Before pressing the jailbreak button, We recommend closing all other apps and turning off WiFI and Cellular Data to increase the success rate of the jailbreak.

If your device reboots, unlock it, and wait at least 30 seconds before pressing the jailbreak button again.
