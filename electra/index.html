Electra or unc0ver?

 

If you're looking to jailbreak with Electra, you must know that it uses Substitute instead of Substrate and requires you to use Sileo over Cydia. 

 

We recommend you use unc0ver on iOS 11 because it has advantages over Electra. Those advantages include more kernel exploits, descriptive errors, more settings, and a log.

If you choose unc0ver, you have two options. unc0ver by pwn20wnd, or unc0ver dark, a fork of unc0ver made by Diatrus and Shady. For a comparison of the two, refer to our unc0ver guide.



Installation (with computer)


This is the preferred method of installing jailbreak apps onto one's device, but there is a method to do it without a computer if needed. If you have a computer, then read on.


To get started, you'll need to install Cydia Impactor on your computer. Simply click the link and install it like any other program. Next, you will need the ipa for Electra from Coolstar's website. Once you have it downloaded, plug your device into your computer, open Impactor and check if your device is seen. It should show as <Device Name> [<UUID>].


Then, go to the menu and choose Device --> Install Package. A file browser should open. Select the ipa for Electra, then follow the instructions. If you run into any errors after entering your Apple ID, read our page on Impactor.


Installation (without computer)


Note: This method is only successful when a signing service has a valid certificate.


On your apple device, navigate to jailbreaks.fun in Safari. Scroll down untill you find Electra1141. Tap on it, then press the blue "GET" button.

After waiting a few seconds a popup should appear asking you if you want to install it. Press install. In a few seconds the app should be on your home screen.

Before you open it, open settings, go to General --> Profiles & Device Management and tap the developer under the heading "ENTERPRISE APP".  Press the blue trust button, and you should now be able to open the app.

Before pressing the jailbreak button, We recommend closing all other apps and turning off WiFI and Cellular Data to increase the success rate of the jailbreak.
