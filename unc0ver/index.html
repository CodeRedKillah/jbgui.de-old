What is unc0ver?

unc0ver does not support A12 devices at this time.



unc0ver is an open-source jailbreak tool for Apple devices with versions iOS 11.0 to 12.1.2. Maintained by pwn20wnd and Sam Bingner, this jailbreak tool uses Cydia Substrate to load, run, and patch your system with tweaks in real time. This tool includes a number of settings to adjust your jailbroken state and diagnose any issues you might have.


You can view the source code of unc0ver here.

Compatibility



All devices except A12 Devices (iPhones XR, XS, XS Max and the 2018 iPad Pros) from iOS 11.0-12.1.2 are supported!

unc0ver or unc0ver dark?


If you're looking to jailbreak with unc0ver, you have two options; the original unc0ver, or unc0ver dark, a fork of the unc0ver project developed by Shady and Diatrus, which adds support for Sileo, and allows you to change the look of your jailbreak application using a theme switcher, with preset options (True Black, Dark Purple, and White).


unc0ver (original) does not offer support for Sileo, or application theming options provided by unc0ver dark.


You can learn more about unc0ver dark here.

Standard Installation


This is the preferred method of installing jailbreak apps onto one's device, but there is a method to do it without a computer if needed. If you don't have a computer, click here.


To get started, you'll need to install Cydia Impactor on your computer. Select your operating system, download, and open the application.


Cydia Impactor, upon opening.


Next, connect your iOS device to your computer using a USB cable. Cydia Impactor should recognize your device and show the connection.


If your device isn't recognized immediately, make sure iTunes is installed.

If iTunes is installed, open iTunes and wait for your device to become recognized.


Once your device has been recognized, download your preferred IPA application file.


  • unc0ver
  • unc0ver dark

After the file has finished downloading, drag-and-drop the file onto the Cydia Impactor window.

Installation (no computer)


Note: This method only works when a signing service has a valid certificate.



On your apple device, navigate to jailbreaks.fun in Safari. Scroll down until you find Chimera. Tap on it, then press the blue "GET" button.

After waiting a few seconds a popup should appear asking you if you want to install it. Press install. In a few seconds the app should be on your home screen.

Before you open it, open settings, go to General --> Profiles & Device Management and tap the developer under the heading "ENTERPRISE APP".  Press the blue trust button, and you should now be able to open the app.
